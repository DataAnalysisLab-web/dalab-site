What We Do

Research Areas

Our research bridges data science and real-world challenges through three core areas.

Process Mining & Applications
Event log analysis · Process discovery · Conformance checking

Process mining extracts knowledge from event logs recorded by information systems, enabling the discovery of real-world process models, conformance checking against normative models, and enhancement of existing processes. DA-Lab applies these techniques to healthcare, manufacturing, and enterprise systems.

Automated process discovery from EHR and clinical data
Declarative process model extraction using LLMs
Conformance checking and deviation analysis
Predictive process monitoring
Multi-perspective process mining
Process optimization in industrial settings
Tools & Frameworks: ProM, PM4Py, Celonis, Disco · Application Domains: Healthcare, Manufacturing, Finance
Text Mining & Applications
NLP · Information extraction · Sentiment analysis

Text mining leverages natural language processing and machine learning to extract structured knowledge from unstructured textual data. DA-Lab focuses on applying these methods to scientific literature, business documents, and social media to support decision-making and knowledge discovery.

Named entity recognition and relation extraction
Topic modeling and document classification
Fine-tuning LLMs for domain-specific tasks
Constraint extraction from natural language
Sentiment and opinion analysis
Knowledge graph construction
Models: BERT, Gemma, LLaMA, GPT · Techniques: LoRA, QLoRA, RAG, Prompt Engineering
Industrial Artificial Intelligence
Smart manufacturing · Anomaly detection · Predictive analytics

Industrial AI applies machine learning and data analytics to manufacturing and industrial systems, enabling smarter decision-making, automated quality control, and predictive maintenance. DA-Lab collaborates with industry partners to develop practical AI solutions for real production environments.

Anomaly detection in manufacturing processes
Predictive maintenance with sensor data
Quality control and defect detection
Digital twin and simulation
Supply chain optimization
Human-robot collaboration analytics
Techniques: Deep Learning, Time-series Analysis, Computer Vision · Domains: Electronics, Semiconductor, Automotive
