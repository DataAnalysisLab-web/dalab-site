Teaching

Courses offered by Prof. Minsu Cho at Kwangwoon University.

Spring 2025
IC4021
Data Mining
Undergraduate 3 Credits

Introduction to data mining concepts, algorithms, and applications. Covers association rule mining, clustering, classification, and pattern recognition with hands-on implementation.

IC6012
Process Mining
Graduate 3 Credits

Advanced course on process mining techniques including process discovery, conformance checking, and process enhancement. Students work with real-world event logs using ProM and PM4Py.

IC3015
Machine Learning
Undergraduate 3 Credits

Fundamentals of machine learning including supervised and unsupervised learning, model evaluation, and neural networks. Python-based with scikit-learn and PyTorch.

IC6024
Natural Language Processing
Graduate 3 Credits

Modern NLP techniques from tokenization to large language models. Covers transformer architectures, fine-tuning, and applications in text classification, NER, and generation.

Fall 2024
IC2010
Introduction to Data Science
Undergraduate 3 Credits

Foundational course covering the data science lifecycle: data collection, preprocessing, exploratory analysis, visualization, and basic modeling using Python and pandas.

IC6030
Industrial AI
Graduate 3 Credits

Application of AI in industrial settings including predictive maintenance, quality control, and supply chain optimization. Case studies from manufacturing and logistics sectors.
